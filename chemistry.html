my background in chemistry

My career began in chemistry, where I obtained a PhD in organic chemistry and conducted research in both the USA and with international teams in Poland.

Fluent in English for professional communication, with workplace proficiency in Swedish.

I am skilled in independently managing and delivering projects on time, balancing efficiency with thoroughness, even in fast-paced environments. My strong collaboration and teamwork skills are complemented by a broad expertise across different fields of chemistry, allowing me to quickly adapt to new research areas and methodologies.

My most recent chemistry projects involved integrating AI algorithms in the synthesis of complex natural products and discovering new chemical transformations.
