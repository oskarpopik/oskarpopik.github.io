about me

Welcome!

My name is Oskar Popik, and I am currently a second-year student of Graphic Design and Web Development at Jönköping University.

I transitioned from a career in chemistry to web development, driven by a passion for detail-oriented problem-solving and creative expression. Whether coding interactive websites or designing visually compelling interfaces, I enjoy the challenge of transforming ideas into functional, engaging experiences.

I have proficiency in JavaScript and HTML/CSS, with hands-on experience using frameworks like Node.js and Handlebars. I am also skilled in managing project work through GitHub, ensuring smooth collaboration and version control. On the design side, I work with tools such as Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop to create intuitive, user-friendly interfaces.

My research background has given me an analytical approach to problem-solving, while my work in design keeps user experience at the forefront. In every project, I strive to find the balance between creativity, structure, and adaptability.
