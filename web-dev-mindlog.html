mindlog

A group project done in collaboration with: Julia Can, Tasa Kriznar and Paula Lucane.

Interactive prototype: link to Figma

Skills: UX/UI design, usability testing

Tools used: Figma

MindLog mobile app was designed to support users in tracking their emotions and mental well-being.

The process began with competitor analysis and interviews with young adults aged 15-30, which provided insights into user needs. These findings informed the creation of personas and low-fidelity wireframes.

Throughout the project, iterative design and usability testing helped refine the app's features, which include a calendar view for emotion tracking and suggestions for managing negative emotions.

Attention was given to the app's aesthetics, using warm colors and a log-based mascot to foster a calm, inviting environment.
