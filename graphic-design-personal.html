Building of a personal brand

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects

The design of my portfolio draws inspiration from the iconic Bauhaus art school, known for its groundbreaking approach to modern art, design, and architecture. The Bauhaus movement redefined design by blending creativity with functionality, emphasizing that aesthetics should serve a purpose. This philosophy inspires my work, because I try to aim for designs that are both visually pleasing and highly practical.

Color Choices

The logo features a striking combination of folly red and piston blue, directly inspired by Bauhaus's bold and vibrant posters. These colors not only echo the school's timeless aesthetic but also create a dynamic visual contrast. Using darken blend mode, I achieved a captivating interplay between the red and blue, resulting in a harmonious blend of colors that adds depth and character to the design.

Typeface

The chosen typeface, Neue Kabel, is a modern geometric typeface that aligns perfectly with the Bauhaus-inspired theme. Its clean, geometric forms allow letters to fit seamlessly together and even overlap, creating a sense of cohesion and structural elegance. This overlapping design enhances the visual appeal while maintaining readability.

Overall Concept

My portfolio integrates geometric typography, vibrant colors, and blending techniques to capture the Bauhaus spirit of innovation and simplicity. The intention behind each design element was to create a user experience that balances visual appeal with functionality.
