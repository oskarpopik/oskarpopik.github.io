corporate logo design

Enhancing visual identity of a company

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Justification for a logo design for PostNord

When analyzing PostNord's recent rebranding, done by strategic branding and design agency Bold, I noticed that while the updated visual identity was modern and effective, it lacked a strong, standalone logo. This felt like a missed opportunity to create a symbol that could succinctly represent the company's core values and resonate with customers, especially as parcel deliveries continue to grow in importance for PostNord, surpassing traditional letter deliveries in significance. Such an omission presented an opportunity to visually encapsulate PostNord's core values of reliability, speed, and innovation.

Design choices for the logo

For my re-brand, I designed a logo centered around a 3D representation of a cube—representing parcels, the backbone of PostNord's business. Using negative space, I incorporated the letters “P” and “N” within the cube, subtly connecting the design to the company's name. The logo's dynamic, motion-like appearance reflects the quick and reliable deliveries PostNord promises, addressing one of the main expectations customers have from a logistics provider.

To ensure consistency with PostNord's recent updates, I retained their established color palette, which is already recognizable and aligns with their Nordic identity. By adding this logo, my goal was to create a memorable visual marker that strengthens the brand, connects with its audience, and reinforces its position as a leader in modern logistics.
