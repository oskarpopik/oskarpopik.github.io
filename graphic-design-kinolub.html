Poster for the 10th International Film Festival for Children and Youth KINOLUB 2025

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

The concept behind a promotional poster for a film festival

There is an upcoming 10th-anniversary edition of the International Film Festival for Children and Youth KINOLUB in Kraków, taking place in 2025.

I created a promotional poster for this event featuring a friendly robot that embodies the magic of cinema. The robot's design incorporates film-related elements: its eyes are movie reels, its body resembles a movie theater with iconic red seats, and it holds a clapperboard, symbolizing creativity and film education - the festival's core values. This vibrant, engaging visual celebrates the festival's spirit and connects with its young audience.
