winter is coming

Every winter begins with a single snowflake

Tools used: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

This motion video captures the essence of winter, beginning with a formation of the first delicate snowflake and transitioning into the first snowfall of the season. The journey continues with breathtaking winter landscapes and dynamic scenes of skiing and snowboarding, enhanced by a carefully chosen soundtrack that perfectly complements the excitement and beauty of winter sports depicted in the video. Designed as a tribute to the best season of the year, the video concludes with a playful use of the snowflake as part of the title text, delivering a hopeful message to all winter enthusiasts.

This project reflects a deep personal passion for skiing and outdoor winter activities, combining dynamic visuals with a creative storytelling approach.
